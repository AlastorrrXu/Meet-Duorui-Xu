2009 - Xinjiang

I was born in Xinjiang and experienced the 2009 riots, which left a deep impression on me and shaped my worldview as a global citizen.

2010 - Shanghai

My family moved to Shanghai, where I grew up and developed my early interest in mathematics and statistics.

2018 - CUHK

I was admitted to The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where I explored a mix of Eastern and Western cultures.

2021 - Russell Investments

My internship at Russell Investments introduced me to data analysis and sparked my passion for applying statistics in the real world.

2022 - Stanford

I joined Stanford as a visiting student, where I experienced the American education system and further developed my research skills.

2023 - UIUC

I transferred to the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), and I am now preparing to pursue my dream of studying at Brown University.
